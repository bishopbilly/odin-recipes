Bolognese

Description

This is the real deal, an authentic recipe for a meat sauce.

This is especially great if you to stove your meat for hours untill it's a soft and falls apart on your fork.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Minced onion
  • Minced celery
  • Minced carrot
  • Ground veal
  • Ground pork
  • Ground beef
  • Finely chopped pancetta bacon
  • Milk
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef broth

    • Steps

    1. Heat olive oil and butter over medium heat.
    2. Saute onion, celery and carrots until soft.
    3. Add veel, pork, beef and pancetta, cook until evenly brown, about 15-20 min.
    4. Pour in milk and cook until evaporated, about 15 min.
    5. Stir in tomato sauce and beef broth.
    6. Cover and simmer 2,5 hours.
